WHO ARE WE?

We at Life Plus company wants to help people who are having a hard time with managing their weight and remembering their daily tasks. As such it is our goal to help those people in need through the creation of this website.

Goals

Health

As we are aware that managing one's health is important, as such our goal is to help maintain the health of the people through calculating their BMI and calorie intake

Record

Many different things are happening around us each day and we tend to forget what we must do for today. Our goal for this website is to help people keep track of their daily activities through writing a journal.
